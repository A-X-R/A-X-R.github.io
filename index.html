A-X-R

AR-Duo is about 44 inches tall and 46 inches wide, made of steel sheet and square tubing, brass sheet and tubing, and cast metal parts powered by two geared-down wheel chair motors running 24 volts on two 12 volt batteries. Run time all day. AR-Duo is a tele-presence robot to allow those at home to experience what being at Maker Faire is like. This robot can accurately and safely drive around outside within relatively large groups of people. AR-Duo can interact with the environment (see, speak, and listen).
